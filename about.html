    Our Story

    Established in 2009, previously known as Dminex, where we focused on carpentry services for residential and commercial design. We have since rebranded to today’s DM+, where we expanded into specializing in residential interior design and build.


    Our goal is to design a unique and innovative solution that
    have a perfect balance between functionality and aesthetics.


    Our clients engage us as we have an in-house carpentry where we can ensure high quality production and end-product at affordable prices. We are also committed to provide the best service to our clients even after design project’s completion.
